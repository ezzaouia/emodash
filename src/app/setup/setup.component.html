Learning Platform SpeakPlus

Emodash has been integrated and deployed on the Web based learning platform called SpeakPlus, dedicated to improving oral communication skills in a foreign language (English, French, and Spanish) using video-conferencing technology (see Figure below).

Participants

Each tutor-learner pair met once a week for a learning session of 30 minutes. Pairs used their own devices (see Table below). Learners booked their learning sessions with their tutors at their own pace.
